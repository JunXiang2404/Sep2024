Explore Bali, Indonesia

Price: MYR 3,800 per person

Duration: 7 days, 6 nights

Bali, known as the Island of the Gods, is a paradise on Earth. With its lush landscapes, stunning beaches, vibrant culture, and exquisite temples, Bali offers an unforgettable experience for travelers. Discover the rich heritage, enjoy delicious local cuisine, and indulge in adventurous activities.

Activities

  • Snorkeling in crystal-clear waters
  • Exploring ancient temples like Uluwatu
  • Hiking Mount Batur for a sunrise view
  • Relaxing on white sandy beaches
  • Experiencing traditional Balinese dance performances
